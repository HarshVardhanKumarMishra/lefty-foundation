About Us

From a Basement Project to a Multi-Center Initiative

The Lefty Foundation was born to create a space that celebrated being different and empowered those who felt 'left behind'.

Arun 'Lefty' Verma, Founder

Started in 2019 by a retired engineer, we began by teaching 5 children in a home basement. Today, the Lefty Foundation is a registered non-profit that has touched the lives of thousands of children across Delhi-NCR, using a unique, joyful learning method to teach English.
